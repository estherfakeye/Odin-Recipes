Description

This classic salad is a vibrant, nourishing dish enjoyed at gatherings, festive occasions, or simply as a comforting homemade meal. It’s a colorful blend of shredded cabbage, carrots, lettuce, sweet corn, baked beans, and juicy tomatoes all layered with love and care. Finished with a drizzle of creamy mayonnaise, it’s both satisfying and refreshing.

Whether served alongside jollof rice, grilled chicken, or enjoyed on its own as a light dish, this salad brings a touch of elegance to any table. Its rich textures, subtle sweetness, and wholesome ingredients make it a timeless favorite in Homes. simple, nutritious, and made to be shared

Ingredients

  • 1 small cabbage (shredded)
  • 2 carrots (grated)
  • 1 cup of sweet corn
  • 2 boiled eggs (sliced)
  • 1 cup of baked beans
  • 1 tomato (diced)
  • Mayonnaise or salad cream
  • Lettuce leaves (optional)

Steps

  1. Wash and prep all vegetables (shred cabbage, grate carrots, dice tomatoes).
  2. Boil eggs until hard, let them cool, then slice.
  3. Layer the ingredients in a bowl: cabbage, carrots, sweet corn, beans, eggs, tomatoes.
  4. Top with mayonnaise or salad cream to taste.
  5. Chill in the fridge for 15–30 minutes before serving.
